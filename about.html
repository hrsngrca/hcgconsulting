About Us

At HCG Consulting, we believe small and medium-sized medical practices are the heart of community healthcare. We help practices harness technology to improve patient outcomes, streamline operations, and earn the quality bonuses they deserve, while always keeping the focus on patient-centered care.

Founder

Harrison Garcia founded HCG Consulting after years of working directly with practices, free clinics, and community health organizations. With expertise in clinical quality reporting, EMR optimization, and quality improvement leadership, Harrison helps teams bridge the gap between delivering excellent care and demonstrating it through reliable, actionable data.
